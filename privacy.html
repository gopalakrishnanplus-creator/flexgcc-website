Scope

This policy applies to website visitors, contact form submissions, and related follow-up communications.

1. Information We Collect

FlexGCC may collect the following categories of information:

  • identity and contact data such as your name, company, email address, and role;
  • business inquiry data such as workflow details, systems involved, and stated business impact;
  • technical data such as basic website, browser, device, or hosting logs generated through normal site operation;
  • communications data from emails or follow-up discussions initiated through the website.

2. How We Use Information

FlexGCC uses collected information to:

  • review and respond to workflow review requests;
  • communicate about prospective services or engagements;
  • operate, secure, and improve the website and associated service workflows;
  • maintain internal records and business communications;
  • comply with legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations.

3. Basis for Processing

Where applicable, FlexGCC processes information because it is necessary to respond to your request, pursue legitimate business interests, comply with legal obligations, or because you have chosen to submit the information voluntarily.

4. How Information Is Shared

FlexGCC does not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers that help operate the website and communication workflows, such as hosting, email delivery, form handling, and productivity providers, subject to appropriate operational controls. Information may also be disclosed if required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect legal rights.

5. Email and Contact Form Handling

Information submitted through the contact form is transmitted to FlexGCC’s designated business email inbox and may be copied back to the email address entered in the form. Please avoid submitting highly sensitive personal data unless specifically requested and appropriate safeguards are in place.

6. Data Retention

FlexGCC retains information for as long as reasonably necessary to manage inquiries, evaluate opportunities, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information and the purpose for which it was collected.

7. Security

FlexGCC uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information handled through the website. No internet transmission or storage system is fully secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

8. International Transfers

Website infrastructure and service providers may process information in jurisdictions outside your own. By using the website and submitting information, you understand that such transfers may occur subject to the controls used by FlexGCC and its providers.

9. Your Choices and Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information held by FlexGCC, or object to certain uses of that information. FlexGCC may need to verify your identity before acting on such a request.

10. Cookies and Similar Technologies

This website may use basic technical tools necessary for hosting, performance, and security. If analytics, tracking, or advertising technologies are introduced later, this policy should be updated to reflect those practices.

11. Third-Party Websites

The website may link to third-party websites or services. FlexGCC is not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties, and you should review their policies independently.

12. Policy Updates

FlexGCC may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

13. Contact

Questions or privacy-related requests may be sent to contact@flexgcc.com.
